Target Audience

The target audience for our site are businesses, residents, and visitors. This includes people of all ages from a variety of backgrounds. These individuals will visit our site looking for information about businesses and the local area, as well as networking opportunities.

Personas

Business Owners

Both established and new business owners will visit the site looking to find more information about the chamber, and the benefits of joining. As well as opportunities to grow their business, network, and become more involved in the community.

Community Members

Longstanding and new residents will visit the site in order to support small businesses in the area, be up to date on community events, and find information about discounts and/or other economic, civic, and educational opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What community events are there?
  • How can I become a member?
  • What resources are there for my business?
  • How can my business be more involved with the community?
  • What educational opportunities are there for business owners or potential business owners?
