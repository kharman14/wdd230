Laie Hawaii Temple

The Laie Hawaii Temple is located on the beautiful Hawaiian Island of Oahu. The Laie Temple was dedicated on November 27, 1919 and rededicated on November 21, 2010. Currently, you can perform ordinances and sessions by appointment. The Laie Temple services include clothing rental, and a nearby distribution center.
