White Space and Clean Design

Quip

getquip.com

I think that Quip uses white space and clean design principles well in their website. Throughout their website they keep their design simple and utilize negative space, which makes their sight easy to read. This also makes finding information very easy and doesn't distract.

Hick's Law

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.com

I choose the church's website because I think they do a very good job of categorizing the data to help consumers know how to manage their site. Their website is not overly cluttered, and only has a few articles and navigation options to other pages.

Visual Hierarchy

Slack

slack.com

I think that the Slack website implements visual hierarchy really well. They do this with well placed and sized text that draw the eye. They also keep the page very simple to guide the eye to the most important parts.
